hi, twin.
first of all, happy birthday. i know i told you the first time we talked that i would be the first to greet you on your birthday, but i wasn’t able to, and i’m really sorry. in the two months that i got to know you, i learned so many things about myself and about life. it really hit me hard because you became the one person i looked forward to talking to every single day. whenever you were there, i genuinely felt complete. and you know, now that we don’t talk anymore, i always find myself wondering what’s missing in me. then i realized—i keep coming back to the same answer. ikaw pala talaga yung kulang. i’m not saying all of this because i want you to come back or to change anything. i just want you to know how much light and comfort you brought into my life. with you, i felt like i already had everything :>>
